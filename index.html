About

STR Analyzer evaluates short-term rental investment properties with detailed financial modeling including cash flow, depreciation, tax savings, and 5-year projections.

Each lookup uses the Claude API and costs approximately $0.01.

Model: claude-opus-4-5

Investor Profile

Calculations assume an active STR investor with material participation (avg stay <7 days). Paper losses from depreciation offset ordinary W-2 income — no passive activity limitation.

Platforms: Airbnb (60%), VRBO (30%), Direct (10%)
